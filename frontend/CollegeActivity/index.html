College Badminton Competiton

22023 March 25, α Dongting Yuan and δ Yuheng Chang organized the first badminton competition of our four undergraduate colleges. Each college actively prepared for the competition and set up the team recently. Our team members contained Hongtian Shen, Dongting Yuan, Shengtao Hu, Shuhan Zhao， and Jingwei Xu. The rule of competition is BO3 (three pairs of double players) and in each competition, you should get 11 scores to win. Our college narrowly competed with the γ college and reach the final competition with the δ college. Without a doubt, δ college which is the most powerful college in badminton won the championship with two powerful foreign aid. At the end of the competition, α Dongting Yuan and β Yingliangping Hu start a friendly competition with the badminton master of the lab seniors. The first badminton competition ended up in the laughter and sweat of us.

2022 World Cup

We bought football posters, national flags of the competing nations, and other decorations to decorate the college, all planned and organized by α SDC. We also posted the competition schedule. The college was quickly engulfed in the World Cup's carnival atmosphere. The World Cup attracted not only the students who enjoy football in their daily lives, but also the seniors, teachers, foreign staff, and even the less interested students, who joined in. The atmosphere was very lively as everyone watched the game together.
